What is

i-Shine 8 CAMPWorkshop?

The iShine 8 Talent Camp is a series of workshops offering different classes for children ages 6-11 years old. The series is divided into five camps — Art, Ballet, Dance, Music, and Theater — with activities designed and developed to nurture your child’s natural gift. To ensure that your child is in capable and credible hands, each camp is mentored by people who are experts in their respective fields with years of experience and incomparable talent.

Now on its 8th year, the iShine Talent Camp aims to develop more gifted children nurtured through the arts by being available in key cities of Luzon.
